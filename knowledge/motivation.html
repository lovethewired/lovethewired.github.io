What protocol do you find most interesting?

Uniswap AMM.. Polymarket prediction markets. Uses ERC1155 conditional tokens. Able to deposit collateral to mint all possible outcomes (A, B, C, ...). These can be traded on the open markets. Once the question result is in, only the outcome can be exchanged for collateral.
